We value your feedback and inquiries. As a global open-source organization, we prioritize transparent communication and community-driven development. Our team is dedicated to building the world's most comprehensive coding reference, and your input is vital to this mission.

If you encounter a technical issue with the website or find an error in our language documentation, please use our official GitHub repository. This ensures that your report is tracked by our engineering team and remains accessible to the developer community.
