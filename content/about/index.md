## About CodingDatafy

**CodingDatafy** is a global, open-source organization founded in 2026. In an era where technology evolves daily, developers need more than just scattered tutorials; they need a structured, high-performance, and version-controlled encyclopedia of technical knowledge.

## Our Roadmap
1. **The Foundation**: Establishing the core architecture and the global index of the top 25 languages.
2. **Deep-Dive Reference**: Developing comprehensive syntax guides for each indexed language.
3. **The Long Tail**: Documenting legacy and emerging experimental languages to complete the world's largest reference.
